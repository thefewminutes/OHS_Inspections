Hanging Bracket Scaffold Working at Heights

Void protection (High Risk)
Is void protection in place with compliance certificate?

Roof Guard Rail and Condition & Access (High Risk)
Is there a drop zone area for roof tillers' spoils?
Roof workers have measures in place to not endanger any people below?
Roof guard railing in place for all roof work?
Roof guard rail has identified access points?
The balcony guard rail is in place for all balcony works?
Do all openings above 2m have guard railing?
e.g. windows, balcony openings (stair risers have hand rails)
Does the balcony and alfresco have sufficient structural support?
eg: structural posts or accrow props for temp support
Do all works near an open edge have sufficient guard rail height?

Trestles
External/internal: are they fully planked, less than 2m in height, have solid stable footings, safe access/egress and a clear fall zone, with correct locking pins?

Ladders (High Risk)
Extension Ladders in good condition no damaged rungs, no greater than 1:4 vertical ratio?
Ladders secure / tied off / extend 900mm past roof line when accessing heights?
All types of ladders are minimum Industrial grade rated at a minimum 120kgs?
Step/platform ladder fully open, both stays in good condition and on stable ground?
Are trades working off step ladders safely?
eg: not standing on top step and not over reaching to complete task. (platform ladders recommended)

Site Conditions / Housekeeping Items of Plant Trenches (High Risk Works)

Items of Plant Trenches (High Risk Works)

Do workers who undertake tasks which require a license/certificate of competency have such a license/certificate?
e.g.: Excavators, skid steer, forklift, crane, boom pumps, spotters, riggers, dogmen
Is there a pre-start checklist completed for the item of mobile plant?
Warning devices operational (reverse beepers, flashing lights, horn)?
Does the plant look fit for purpose or use?
e.g.: Earthmover quick hitches secured/pinned
Open screw pile holes and piers that are left unattended have covers preventing entry and identified on builder's site sign?
Large exposed trenches have fencing/barrier in place, Trenches in minimum excess of 1.5m deep have been battered back and/or shored?
Soil pile located minimum 1m from edge of trench?

Electrical Tools and Equipment

Must be tested prior to first use or on installation
Site Meter Box RCD test date
If a generator is used, does it have an RCD fitted and not used indoors or in confined space?
Electrical equipment (leads and tools) tested & tagged every 3 months and in date?
Are electrical leads exiting through the bottom of the meter box?
Are electrical leads raised up where practicable and minimum 15amp leads (1.5mm)?
